    Selena Singleton

    Selena Singleton is a native of Savannah, Ga and always had the ambition to pursue the foundation for higher learning. Her academic journey began while still in High School, Selena joined Savannah State University's Upward Bound project and became a dual enrolled student. During her Sophomore year she followed her passion to Georgia Southern University where she graduated with a B.S in Fashion Merchandising. Upon graduating, Selena completed her internship at Belk, inc where she learned and developed leadership skills and how to implement strategies that were efficient in reaching goals.

    Throughout her professional career, Selena has remained true to her ideals, working through uncertainties. In her most recent position at Comcast,the dynamics of IT peaked her interest and it led Selena to invest and take both her professional and technical skillset to the next level. She is excited to apply all of the knowledge that she has gained from the Georgia Tech coding Bootcamp into the next phase of her career as a Web Developer.
